Fugazi

Musical style

Their inventive, interlocking guitarwork often defies the traditional notion of "lead" and "rhythm" guitars and features and dissonant chords and progressions filtered through a hardcore punk lens.

Later, Fugazi more fully integrated elements of punk rock, hardcore, soul and noise with an inventively syncopated rhythm section.
